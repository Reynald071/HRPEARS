About

Find Out More About Us

The DSWD My PORTAL is a combination of systems and processes that connect personnel administration, human resource management and information technology through an innovative software design.

The Future for DSWD My PORTAL

PORTAL is designed and being developed to address the growing needs of the DSWD Field Office Caraga, its staff and its clientele. The new system will be more efficient and effective

  • In Terms of Speed

    Django is ridiculously fast and was designed to help developers take applications from concept to completion as quickly as possible.

  • Performance

    Django takes security seriously and helps developers avoid many common security mistakes. Some of the busiest sites on the Web leverage Django's ability to quickly and flexibly scale.

  • Data Management

    The main benefits of using Django ORM (Object-Relational Mapper) instead of SQL is a huge improvement in development speed, code maintenance, security and ease of development in general.

  • Better Service Delivery to its users.

    DSWD My PORTAL provides a lot of services like Issuances, Downloadable Forms, Personal Data Sheet, Announcements, Travel Management, Payroll Management and more.

The system will also be using the Single Sign-On technology for security and authentication. PORTAL is being developed using the Django Framework, a high-level Python Web Framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design. It is expected to be ridiculously fast, secure and exceedingly scalable.

Services

PORTAL provides us

Profiling and Staffing

This includes personal data sheet, employee separation, employee accomplishment, daily time records, and health checklist.

Payroll Management

Administration of the financial record of employee's salaries, wages, bonuses, net pay, and deductions.

Leave Management

Process of managing employee time-off requests in a fair, accurate, and efficient way.

Travel Management

This includes travel requisition, generation of travel orders, and manages employee travel history.

Awards Management

This includes nominations, deliberation and posting of declared winners on specific awards.

Overtime and CTDO Requests (New)

This includes filing of overtime and ctdo requisition on the system.

Announcement, Issuances, and Downloadables

Posting of announcement, issuances, and downloadables.

Grievances (New)

Filing, forwarding, and acted on grievances

Document Tracking and Transmittal (New)

Tracking and transmittal of documents.
